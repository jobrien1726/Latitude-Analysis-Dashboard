Wind Speed

We can see from the above plot that Wind Speed tends to significantly less at the equator (0 Deg. Latitude) and increases as we move further from the equator. The trade winds coming from the south and the north meet near the equator. These converging trade winds produce general upward winds as they are heated, so there are no steady surface winds. This area of calm is called the doldrums.
